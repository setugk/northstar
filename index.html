Getting Started
1 Download the template, or create a plain text file in any notes app
2 Add your goals and tasks using this format:
Goal: Learn to cook

Task: Pick 5 recipes
Time: 30 min
Link: https://example.com
Notes: Start simple
3 Tap Add goals to load it into the app
Northstar tracks goals over a quarter — it's not a to-do list. Be intentional: craft your goals in a doc, upload when ready. You can't edit inside the app, and that's by design.
